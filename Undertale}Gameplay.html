Gameplay


There's a very cool gameplay.There are 3 rodes: Pacifist, Neutral and Genocide.
In Pacifist, you need to spare everyone.In Neutral, you can choose who to kill and who to spare.
And Genocide.You need to kill everyone.You must walk in the room to find the monsters to kill them until no one left in this location.
There's 6 locations and 60 endings, because every action that you do can change everything.
